Eyeballing Tips

for when you've lost the measuring cups

🤲A "handful" is roughly ½ cup

For pasta, grains, or greens, one cupped handful is about a half cup. Two hands is a cup.

🫙A "glug" of oil is ~2 tbsp

A quick pour around the pan — not a tiny dribble, not a flood. You'll get a feel for it fast.

🧂Salt generously, taste often

Season in layers: pasta water, during cooking, and at the end. Taste before you add more.

🔥Medium-high is your best friend

Most sautéeing happens at medium-high. If nothing's sizzling, turn it up. If it's smoking, turn it down.
